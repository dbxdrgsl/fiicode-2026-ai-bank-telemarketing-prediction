INTERPRETATION
============================================================

Sample weights are computed as: weight = 1 / (1 - p_test)
Where p_test is the probability of being from the test set.

High weight => sample looks like test data => weight it more.
Low weight => sample looks unlike test data => weight it less.

This reweighting should help the model focus on training samples
that are most representative of the test distribution.
